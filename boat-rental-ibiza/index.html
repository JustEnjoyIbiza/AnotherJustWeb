Boat rental Ibiza: FAQ

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST TO RENT A BOAT IN IBIZA?

Day charters at Just Enjoy Ibiza start from €762/day for a Quicksilver 875 and go up to €12,950/day for the Riva Argo 90. Most popular boats sit between €1,000 and €3,500.

Fuel is charged separately and depends on the route — typically €100–€400 per day.

DO I NEED A LICENSE TO RENT A BOAT IN IBIZA?

No license needed if you charter with a professional captain — the option most clients choose. Some smaller boats can be rented without a captain if you hold a valid recreational nautical license recognized by Spain.

CAN I RENT A BOAT IN IBIZA FOR HALF A DAY?

Yes — many of our boats are available for 4-hour morning (10:00–14:00) or afternoon (15:00–19:00) charters. Half-day rates are roughly 60–70% of a full day. WhatsApp us for the current price on a specific boat.

HOW FAR IN ADVANCE SHOULD I BOOK?

For July and August, book at least 4–6 weeks in advance — premium boats sell out fast. May, June and September are easier, often bookable 1–2 weeks out. Last-minute is sometimes possible — always worth asking.

WHAT IF THE WEATHER IS BAD?

Your safety comes first. If the port authority issues a warning or our captain judges it unsafe, we reschedule at no extra cost or refund your deposit if rescheduling isn’t possible during your stay.
